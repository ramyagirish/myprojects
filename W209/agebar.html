This visualization was created to gain insights on the yearly and state-wise trends in AIDS infection rates and cases in terms of the key demographic characteristic of Age. By pressing on individual bars we could select the set of states and years by which the rates or the total cases could be aggregated.

Important Instructions

In order to activate filters and view the tables, the following button should be pressed each time the visualization is opened.

Activate Filter

You could view the selected rows by clicking on the following buttons.

Rates Table Cases Table
